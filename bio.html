Paean Luby

Informatics

Indiana University Bloomington

Paean Luby is a first-year Ph.D. student in Informatics at Indiana University Bloomington studying the intersection of neuroscience, immersive technology, and cognitive stimulation under Dr. Patrick Shih. She holds a B.S. in Psychology and Computer Science from the University of Richmond, where she also completed research in virtual reality and pre-clinical behavioral neuroscience. Her work is informed by personal experiences observing both cognitive decline and meaningful engagement, motivating her commitment to accessible, evidence-based approaches to brain health.
