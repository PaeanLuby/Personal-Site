My name is Paean (pronounced like payin' the bills), and I am a senior at the University of Richmond. I am also an aspiring virtual and augmented software engineer with a hope to eventually impart this experience as a computer science professor. Specifically, after my grandparents’ deaths from dementia and stroke, I’ve dedicated my schooling to slowing neurodegenerative diseases using digitally simulated enriched environments. My particular focus is on the Latino community, who are among those least likely to seek assistance despite their being disproportionately affected. For fall 2025, I hope to gain acceptance into a Ph.D. program in Informatics or Computer Science.
