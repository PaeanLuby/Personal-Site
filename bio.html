My name is Paean (pronounced like payin’ the bills), and I’m an incoming Ph.D. student at Indiana University Bloomington. After losing two of my grandparents to dementia and stroke, I’ve dedicated my academic journey to combating neurodegenerative diseases through immersive, digitally simulated enriched environments. I aim to integrate this approach with neuroengineering principles to develop engaging, evidence-based rehabilitation software that supports cognitive and neural recovery.
